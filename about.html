BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a seven-member South Korean boy band formed by Big Hit Entertainment. The name subsequently became a backronym for Beyond The Scene. On June 12, 2013, they performed the song "No More Dream" from their initial album 2 Cool 4 Skool to commemorate their debut on June 13, 2013. BTS rose to fame with their hottest comeback Blood, Sweat and Tears, which caugh the attention of Local and International fans alike. After that they continued their sucess in the states by apperaing on TV Shows, Interviews and etc. BTS is labeled as one of the most important and sucessful groups to come out of K-pop
